FAQ - Curious minds want to know

Here are answers to your burning questions.

What is IDigitalPreneur?

IDigitalPreneur is an online platform that offers a wide range of digital courses and skill based trainings to help individuals enhance their skills and knowledge in all the fields relevant in today’s time.

Are the courses self-paced?

Yes, all of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to study at your own convenience. You can access the course materials anytime, from anywhere, and progress through the content at a pace that suits you best.

Can I access the course materials after I complete the course?

Absolutely! Once you enroll in a course, you'll have lifetime access to the course materials. This means you can revisit the content, review the lessons, and access any future updates or additions to the course.

Do you offer any certifications?

Yes, you receive organisational certification on the completion of courses. Not only this but our courses are also MSME certified which enhance the credibility of the courses multiple folds. Upon successful completion of these courses, you'll receive a certificate that validates your achievement. Certificates can be used to enhance your professional profile and showcase your skills to potential employers or clients.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in a course?

The prerequisites vary depending on the course. Some courses may require prior knowledge or experience in a specific area, while others are designed for beginners. The course descriptions will provide information about any prerequisites or recommended background knowledge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment platforms. Simply choose your preferred payment method during the checkout process.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we take the security of your payment information seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure that your payment details are protected.

Do you offer refunds?

We have a refund policy in place. If you are not satisfied with a course, you may be eligible for a refund within a specific period. Please refer to our refund policy for detailed information on eligibility and the refund process.

Can I contact support if I have questions or issues?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated support team ready to assist you. If you have any questions, technical difficulties, or require assistance, you can reach out to our support team through the contact information provided on our website.

How do these courses help me start my freelance journey?

Upon completing a course and mastering a specific skill, you can kickstart your freelancing career. There are various websites like Fiverr, Freelancer, Upwork, and many more where you can create your freelance profile and begin working on different projects. This can actually help you make a good amount of money.

How can IDIGITALPRENEUR assist in my freelancing journey?

When you join IDIGITALPRENEUR, you'll find a freelancing section in your dashboard where you can submit projects. Only the top and best portfolios are chosen, so aim to be the best.
